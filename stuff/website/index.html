League of Legends is a 5v5 MOBA(Multiplayer Online Battle Arena), where each team has to work together in order to destroy the enemy nexus, located in their base. The game consists of 3 lanes, with minions/creeps going down each lane, which you can kill to get gold. Gold is used to buy items that enhance your stats and make you do more damage, live longer, or attack faster. There are also 3 towers per lane per team, and two towers protecting the nexus. At the beginning of each lane on each team, there is an inhibitor, which the enemy can destroy, which spawns super minions down that lane that have enhanced health and damage.
